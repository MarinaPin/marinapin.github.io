ABOUT ME

Electronic Engineer with Master degree in Biomedical Engineering

I am Marina, Brazilian but living in Seattle for now. I am passionate about technology and making technology improve peoples' life. Beyond that I also fascinated about design, and how it helps data understanding and costumer’s interaction. I've learned design tools as a hobby but now I'm immersing myself in the world of data visualization and I'm still looking through this new possibility.
In my free time I like hiking and visiting new places. I really like to be outside and admire everything that surrounds me. Also, I enjoy dancing fórro and going out with friends.

Marina Marques


I had the opportunity to be part of really interesting and innovative projects, all them focusing in bringing new useful technologies with low cost. We succeed in building a new medical device for cancer treatment that is now in a clinical trial. The team I worked with was quite interdisciplinary, such as health professionals, biologists, government agencies and entrepreneurs.
After a while we wondered... "We built functional and helpful devices here, why don't we sell them ourselves?" and this was the beginning of the startup called Unite do Care. A startup focused on sharing the technologies we have created on the LaB with patients and costumers.
My abilities include: technical tools (data analysis, design, writing, publish, manage, social media), mechanical manufacturing, shape-memory alloys, image processing, hardware development and technical standards for electronic medical devices.
My greatest interest is to improve technologies to solve real problems. I am always looking for opportunities to make it real.
